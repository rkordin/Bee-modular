CONFIGURATIONS

What a shape can become

What follows is not a catalogue. It is a set of possibilities. Eight, for now.

The question we kept asking: what do people actually need from a building? Not what architects want to design. Not what developers want to sell. What do you need — today, and in the version of your life you haven't met yet.

The hexagonal frame is always the same. The life inside it is yours.

BEE.cozy

Home

A micro-home with glass on three sides and the world on the fourth. Oak floors underfoot, walnut paneling at your back, rainfall shower after the hike, underfloor heating before the coffee.

Start with one module. Add another when the family grows. The frame accepts new frames. Home from the first evening.

StructureGalvanized steel frame, 3-side panoramic glazing, triple-glazed low-E (Ug ≤ 0.6 W/m²K)
InteriorEngineered oak flooring, walnut veneer, integrated kitchen, full bathroom
ClimateUnderfloor hydronic heating, heat pump, ventilation with ≥85% heat recovery
Off-gridSolar roof, lithium battery 10-20 kWh, rainwater UV filtration, composting
ExpandModules connect via weathersealed corridor; plumbing cross-connect <4h

BEE.guest

Hospitality

A hotel room that doesn't need a hotel. Glass-fronted, timber-lined, fully self-contained. Deploy three along a ridge. Add five more when the reviews come in.

If a location underperforms, reposition — the module moves, the investment doesn't disappear. Revenue from day one. Literally.

Revenue€315/m²/year, 72% occupancy, >25% net yield (observed)
ConfigSelf-contained unit with ensuite, kitchenette, climate, private terrace
DeploySingle, cluster, or courtyard; shared infrastructure modules available
RepositionRelocatable by flatbed; full reinstallation within 48 hours

BEE.well

Wellness

A Finnish sauna with panoramic glass and nothing between you and the tree line. Wood-fired or electric — both options, no compromise on either.

Yoga at dawn, massage at noon, sauna at dusk. Wellness should not require a journey. It should be where you already are.

SaunaFinnish design, wood-fired or electric, 80-110°C, panoramic glazed wall
ConfigsSauna, yoga studio, spa treatment, cold plunge annex, or combined
InteriorHeat-treated aspen/alder, non-slip stone in wet areas
ConnectPairs with BEE.cozy or BEE.guest; standalone equally supported

BEE.safe

Protection

Not a bunker. A room. On ordinary days it is a well-built room with good acoustics and a solid door. Your children do homework here.

Underneath: Armox 500T steel, Kevlar liner, HEPA H14 filtration. We target VPAM PM 9 ballistic and STANAG 2280 blast ratings. Because preparation is not pessimism. It is responsibility.

OuterArmox 500T, ~10.5mm, ~500 HBW (SSAB certified)
CoreS355J2 steel frame, full perimeter welded
InnerKevlar aramid composite — spall containment
BallisticTarget VPAM PM 9 (7.62×51mm AP)
HVACHEPA H14 (99.995%), positive pressure, CBRN option

BEE.cellar

Wine & Archive Storage

Wine asks very little. Constant temperature. Stable humidity. Darkness. No vibration. But it asks it absolutely.

Partially buried, naturally temperature-stable year-round. Your 2015 Barolo will be fine in 2045.

InstallPartially or fully buried; earth-pressure-rated frame
Climate12-14°C ±0.5°C, 60-70% RH ±3%
