How it works

Six stages. Clear deliverables. A decision point at every step.

You will never wonder what happens next.

Twenty-eight years of manufacturing practice, applied to a straightforward question: how do you want to live?

01 — Discovery

A conversation. Not a presentation.

We listen more than we talk. The most important thing is not what you want to build. It is how you want to live.

So we start there. Not in a conference room. On your hillside. Feeling the wind. Seeing where the light falls at four in the afternoon. Understanding the slope, the access road, the neighbour's tree line.

Context matters more than most people think.

02 — Configuration

Not design. Configuration.

We propose: number of modules, layout, orientation, exterior skin, interior finish, building systems. You see it in 3D before a single sheet of steel is cut.

This is not design. Design implies invention from scratch. This is configuration. The hexagonal structural system exists. The connection details are proven. We are adapting it to your life.

03 — Confirmation

Review. Refine. Confirm. Specification locked. Layout final. Price fixed.

No surprises. No scope creep. No "we discovered something on site and it costs more." The factory knows exactly what it builds. You know exactly what you receive.

04 — Production

Krško, Slovenia. A factory floor, not a building site.

CNC-cut steel. EN 729-3 certified welding. Every weld documented. Every dimension verified against the model. Interiors fitted in controlled conditions. No rain. No mud. No wind delays.

A great cabinet maker finishes the back of a drawer. Even though it will never be seen. We finish what you will never see. Because what you do when no one is looking is the truest measure of who you are.

FacilityISO 9001 facility. Structural steel S355J2, CNC plasma cut, welding per EN 1090-2.
TestingNon-destructive testing on critical joints. Dimensional inspection at each stage.
InsulationMineral wool + PIR composite, U-values to specification.
QAAir tightness, electrical continuity, plumbing pressure test. Full photo documentation.
OutcomeCompleted modules. Test documentation package. Shipping clearance.

05 — Delivery

From factory to site. Straightforward logistics.

Two modules per truck. Standard transport, standard roads, standard construction equipment on site. No specialist cranes. No wide-load permits for standard modules.

ISO 1161 corner nodes — the same connection system used in global shipping. Proven. Simple. Strong.
