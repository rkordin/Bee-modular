UNAPOLOGETICALLY HIGH-SPEC

Defence-grade engineering meets architectural beauty. The outer shell: Armox 500T armour steel, ~10.5 mm, ~500 HBW. The structural core: S355 steel. The inner layer: Kevlar anti-spall liner. HVAC with HEPA H14 filtration. Overpressure capability. Optional CBRN systems.

Armox 500T~500 HBWKevlar linerHEPA H14CBRN ready

The outer shell utilizes Armox 500T armour steel — approximately 10.5 mm thickness with hardness around 500 HBW — identical material specification to military vehicle armour, applied to civilian protection.

Environmental control systems transform the module into a sealed micro-atmosphere. HVAC with HEPA H14 filtration removes 99.995% of particles. Overpressure capability prevents external contamination. Optional CBRN systems address chemical, biological, radiological, and nuclear threat scenarios.

The frame follows ISO logic. ISO 1161 corner nodes enable standardized lifting. Transport-optimized dimensions allow two units per standard truck. Deployment: 24 hours from arrival to operational.
