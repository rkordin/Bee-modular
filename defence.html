Not a bunker

BEE.safe

BEE.safe is a room. A beautiful, functional, light-filled room that happens to stop bullets. Filter air. Maintain pressure. Hold steady when nothing else does.

On ordinary days it is a well-built space with good acoustics and solid walls. You work in it. Read in it. Your children do homework in it.

Behind the timber lining: Armox 500T armour steel. Beneath the plaster: Kevlar anti-spall layer. Behind the vent covers: HEPA filtration rated for pharmaceutical clean rooms.

Every BEE.safe module is finished to the same standard as our living spaces. Same timber options. Same acoustic treatment. Same lighting design.

The protective layers are structural — they do not dictate the interior. Clients select finishes, furniture layouts, and lighting exactly as they would for any room.

Because it is a room. It just also happens to be one of the most protected spaces you can own.

Environmental Control

A sealed room that cannot breathe is not a safe room. It is a different kind of danger. Air matters. Temperature matters. Pressure matters. CO² concentration matters.

HEPA H14 filtration — pharmaceutical clean room standard. Captures 99.995% of particles at 0.3 microns. Positive pressure ensures air flows out, never in.
